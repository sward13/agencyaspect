We are excited to launch Agency Aspect with two beautiful hybrid bags. Designed with ergonomic shapes, they conform to your body and keep you hands-free. Design leaders Liana Delucca Johnson and Michelle Rose have created the perfect pairing of performance and luxury for today’s badass woman.

Both bags combine gorgeous supple leathers, found in the most beautifully made handbags, with technical CORDURA® Brand fabrics known in the industry for extreme durability and strength.

Anodized aircraft-grade aluminum hardware provides lightweight strength and an electric pop of color.

Matte black hardware and waterproof zippers lend sophistication to ultimate performance.

Hybrid Messenger
This bag is the perfect size for work and commute. Our signature double front pocket has a magnet closure plus a hidden zippered pocket. Exterior side snap pockets can fit large water bottles and two exterio leather loops are perfect for clipping keys.

A padded back pocket fits a 15” Laptop or a larger iPAD PRO and is easily accessible at airport checkpoints. A pass through panel fits over luggage handles and a detachable crossbody strap is included for bike or board commuting.

A leather handle in addition to crossbody adjustable straps allow multiple ways to carry.

Padded details in a beautiful textured saffiano leather provide durbaility and ensure this bag will last and look amazing for many years.

Hybrid Crossbody
This smaller crossbody is the perfect size for traveling or everyday. It has our signature double front pocket and is made from a luxurious soft and durable smooth leather.

Two side pockets with our logo matte black ring snap help secure a small water bottle, sunglasses or keys.

A large back pocket fits a notebook or small tablet and two hook and loop interior pockets hold business cards. Our signtaure pop color lining will feature a beautfiul embossed texture and ensure that you can always find your keys!

This crossbody style is the perfect badass update to your leather purse. It features a top handle just like the messenger and the adjustable strap can be worn crossbody or over-shoulder when you want to be completely handsfree.

Our story

We met while leading design teams at The North Face. Michelle led women’s outerwear design, where she drove innovation in materials, fit and color, and championed the merging of performance and style. Liana thrived in equipment where she designed popular backpacks, bags and accessories, and specialized in women’s fit and ergonomics. We bonded over our shared passion for designing beautiful technical products for women.

We started Agency Aspect to pursue our vision for women’s equipment and apparel that fosters freedom and movement in every aspect of our lives. We’ve spent the past two years designing and refining our first collection. We are excited to bring it to you!
